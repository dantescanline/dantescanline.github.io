My Greenhouse

This is my collection of other people's webgardens. A webgarden is a small 250px by 250px website you create and host on your own site. Then other people (like me) can embed it with a simple iframe. I stole the layout for mine from melonking. The background images are from the Oakland Columnbarium, a very beautiful space designed by architect Julia Morgan. She named the main building 'The chapel of the chimes'

Read more about web gardens at miss moss' site

This is my webgarden

You can embed it in your greenhouse by copying this iframe code:

<iframe src="https://dantescanline.com/webgarden.html" height="250px" width="250px" scrolling="no"></iframe>
