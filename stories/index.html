Stories I Like

I Decided to make copies of stories I like so I could easily give them to other people.



The Machine Stops by E. M. Forster, 1909

While this can certainly be read at face value as an anti-technology fable, I think it's deeper than that. Uncanny technological predictions for it's publication year.


Mimsy Were the Borogoves by Lewis Padgett, 1943

An inventor thoughtlessly throws a box of futuristic toys back in time to 1942. I spent years as a child wishing I had these toys myself.


Hardfaught by Gear Bear, 1983

An intergalactic war with aliens stretches over eons and becomes itself alien. Call this a short novella based on the lenght.
